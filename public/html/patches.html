What do the version numbers mean?

1.0.0 - the number here (underlined number) is the major version number.
1.0.0 - the number here is the minor version number.
1.0.1 - the number here is the patch version.

What constitutes a Version / Patch?

A major version is a version with massive overhauls and such as full CSS reworks, total refactoring, full theme changes, etc... A minor version is just a version that adds or removes a few small things, and doesn't change the entire website completely (ex: small UI additions constitute a "minor" change) A patch is just whenever I need to fix something real quick or when I add one part of a minor version to test things out
